Empowering Educators, Inspiring Students

We travel across South Africa, equipping educators with the skills to coach rugby, uplifting the image of schools, and creating job opportunities for local communities.

Our Mission

To provide coaching seminars and basic rugby knowledge to high school educators, enabling them to coach students effectively. Our goal is to uplift schools, boost student self-esteem, and create opportunities for rugby coaches, referees, and local residents.
